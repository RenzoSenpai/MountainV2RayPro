Mountain V2Ray Pro Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Feb 14, 2026

Mountain V2Ray Pro ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we may disclose it.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to ensure the proper functioning and security of our VPN service:

  • Device ID: We collect your device's unique identifier to prevent unauthorized access to our VPN. This helps us maintain the security of your connection and prevent misuse of our service.
  • Device Model: We collect your device model to ensure compatibility with our VPN app and provide optimal performance.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any data that can be used to track your online activity, including:

  • Browsing history: We do not track the websites you visit while connected to our VPN.
  • Traffic destination: We do not monitor the destination of your internet traffic.
  • Data content: We do not access or store the content of your data transfers.
  • DNS queries: We do not log your DNS queries to protect your privacy.

Use of Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the VPN service: We use your device ID to verify your authorization and ensure a secure connection. We use your device model to optimize the VPN app's performance for your device.
  • Preventing unauthorized access: We use device IDs to identify and prevent unauthorized attempts to access our VPN service.

Sharing of Information

We do not share any of the information we collect with third parties. We may only disclose information if required by law or to comply with a legal process.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can uninstall the Mountain V2Ray Pro app at any time to stop us from collecting information from your device.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website or within the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: mountainv2ray@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy is compliant with the following principles:

  • Transparency: We clearly explain what information we collect and how we use it.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect the information necessary to provide the VPN service.
  • Purpose Limitation: We use the collected information only for the stated purposes.
  • Security: We take steps to protect the information we collect.
  • User Control: You have the choice to uninstall the app.
